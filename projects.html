PROJECT VALVES

An industrial design project focused on reinventing fluid control systems with a blend of robust engineering and sleek, modern aesthetics.

CLIENT

SD Valves

SERVICES

Product Design, CAD Modeling, Prototyping

YEAR

2024

The Challenge

SD Valves, a global leader in fluid motion and control, tasked us with designing a revolutionary smart valve for the petrochemical industry. The goal was to create a valve that not only offered superior performance and reliability but also integrated advanced IoT capabilities for real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance. The design had to be both iconic and functional, capable of withstanding harsh environments while providing intuitive user interaction.

Our Approach

Our process began with extensive research into the daily operations and pain points of technicians in the field. We adopted a human-centered design approach, focusing on creating a product that was not only technologically advanced but also easy to use and maintain. The design language was inspired by armored vehicles and high-performance machinery, resulting in a rugged, yet sophisticated aesthetic. We utilized advanced CAD software and finite element analysis (FEA) to ensure structural integrity and optimal flow dynamics. Multiple 3D-printed prototypes were created for ergonomic testing and user feedback sessions, allowing us to refine the design iteratively.

The final product features a modular design for easy customization, a user-friendly digital interface with a high-contrast display, and wireless connectivity for remote operation and data analysis. The use of corrosion-resistant materials and a sealed enclosure ensures durability in extreme conditions. The result is the Flowserve 'Axiom' smart valve, a product that sets a new standard in the industry for performance, usability, and design.
