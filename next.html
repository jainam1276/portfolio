PROJECT BUTTERFLY VALVES

A specialized industrial design project focused on creating high-performance butterfly valves for critical applications, merging durability with precision engineering.

The Challenge

SD Valves, a respected manufacturer of industrial valves, approached us to design a new line of butterfly valves. The primary challenge was to enhance performance for high-pressure environments while improving the valve's lifespan and reducing maintenance requirements. The design needed to be robust, easily identifiable as an SD Valves product, and optimized for manufacturing efficiency.

CLIENT

SD Valves

SERVICES

Industrial Design, CAD Modeling, CFD Analysis

YEAR

2024

Our Approach

Our design process was rooted in engineering fundamentals. We started with computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations to analyze and optimize the flow characteristics of the valve disc. This data-driven approach allowed us to minimize turbulence and pressure drop, leading to a more efficient valve. We selected high-strength stainless steel alloys and advanced sealing materials to ensure durability under extreme conditions. The aesthetic was kept clean and functional, reflecting the product's purpose-driven nature.

The Result

The final design features a unique double-offset disc that reduces wear on the seal, significantly extending the valve's operational life. The actuator mounting flange was standardized for easy integration with various automation systems. We delivered a complete design package, including detailed manufacturing drawings and assembly procedures, enabling SD Valves to seamlessly transition into production. The 'SDV-BFY' series sets a new benchmark for performance and reliability in their product portfolio.
